Cookies Privacy Policy

At Pegasus Construction Services, LLC, we are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of our users. This Cookies Privacy Policy outlines why and how we collect cookies when you visit our website.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They serve various purposes, including enhancing your browsing experience, remembering your preferences, and providing anonymous statistical data to help us improve our website.

Why We Collect Cookies

We collect cookies to improve the functionality and usability of our website and to provide you with a personalized experience. Here are the main reasons why we collect cookies:

  • Website Functionality: Cookies enable essential functions of our website, such as remembering your language preference, login credentials (if applicable), and preferences for viewing certain content.
  • Analytics and Performance: We utilize cookies to gather anonymous data about how visitors interact with our website. This information helps us analyze website traffic, identify popular pages, and make informed decisions to enhance our website's performance and user experience.
  • Marketing and Personalization: Cookies allow us to deliver targeted advertisements and personalized content based on your interests and browsing behavior. By collecting cookies, we can tailor our marketing efforts to provide you with relevant information about our services and promotions.
  • Third-Party Services: We may use third-party services, such as social media platforms and analytics tools, that utilize cookies to enhance their functionality and provide integrated features on our website. These cookies are subject to the respective third parties' privacy policies.

Managing Cookies

You have the option to control and manage cookies in your browser settings. You can choose to accept or reject cookies or set preferences for specific types of cookies. However, please note that disabling certain cookies may impact the functionality and user experience of our website.

By continuing to use our website, you consent to the collection and use of cookies as described in this Cookies Privacy Policy.
